Welcome to the Design Innovation and Intelligent Manufacturing (DIIM) Lab

The DIIM Lab, established by Dr. Sheng Yang in September 2020, is affiliated with the College of Engineering at the University of Guelph in Guelph, ON, Canada. We are a multidisciplinary research team working across mechanical engineering, computer engineering, and data science.
