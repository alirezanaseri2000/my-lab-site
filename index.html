Welcome to the Design Innovation and Intelligent Manufacturing (DIIM) Lab

The DIIM Lab, established by Dr. Sheng Yang in September 2020, is part of the College of Engineering at the University of Guelph. We are a multidisciplinary research team spanning mechanical engineering, computer engineering, and data science. Our mission is to develop next‑generation intelligent engineering systems through synergistic research on AI, digital twins, and human‑centered design.
